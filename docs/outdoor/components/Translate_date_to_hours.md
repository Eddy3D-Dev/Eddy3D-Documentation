## ![](../images/icons/Translate_date_to_hours.png) Translate date to hours

![](../images/components/Translate_date_to_hours-crop.png)

Translate Ladybug analysis period to hours of the year. Eddy3D 0.5.0.815

#### Input
* ##### AP 
Analysis Period (Ladybug format). The following would for example list hours from the second hour and second day of February until the 11th hour on the 11th day of November: (2,2,2) (11,11,11)

#### Output
* ##### H
Hours from Analysis Period.
